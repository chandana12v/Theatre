Stage

Preparation of the stage

Stage measurements :

Width: 17ft + 26ft + 17ft (total = 60ft)

Height: 15ft + 3ft

Depth: 35ft

The roof of the stage must be equiped with horizontal and vertical rulers and should be stronger enough to bare 1.5 tons of weight. The curtains and other trick scene equipment woud be hanged to the roof.

Makeup

All this group members care keen about the deatails of the makeup and costumes. All artists can makeup themselves. Often they all are helping each other.

Education

The children’s primary education is the education about the Drama. When it comes to general education, children attend the classes in the morning and perform on the stage in the evenings. There are kids who study the primary, secondary educations, and there are young people who are pursuing post graduations, and PhD’s., in Theatre Arts., Some of the young artists who are studying their post graduations, have to go a 100kms daily to have the classes at the college., and when they return in the evening, they don’t miss to perform on the stage.

living rooms

These temporary shelters are the living rooms. Thou government has provided some colony quarters, they have to stick to the stage. The rooms are all attached to the stage to quickly get on to the stage.
