Sri Venkateswara Surabhi Theatre - Hyderabad

Sri Venkateswara Surabhi Theatre is a rare institution of family theatre with 60 family members, Surabhi theatre has unbroken record of 135 years. It is famous for its Padya Natakam (The classical verse play) performances adorned with colorful illusionary backgrounds, sets and trick scenes. The interesting thing about this troupe is that all artists/technicians of this troupe are from one family, dedicated their lives to drama. There are no age limitations or retirements for the artists of this family..
